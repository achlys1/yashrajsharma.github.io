Kalyug Ke Shatranj Mein

Is Kalyug ke shatranj mein, Krishna ki Leela bhari hai, Ram ke niyamon ka shasan, Is abhimaani Kalyug par jaari hai. Apne netra-patal par dekho, Kalyug ka charitra dikh jayega, Krishna ki kriya ka sagar, Saanson mein ghul jayega. Kahne ko to pyade hain hum, Jinda jalti lashein hain hum, Kitna hai abhiman to dekho, Apne andar Ram to dekho. Kalyug ke is shatranj mein, Krishna ki nirmal vaani hai, Karna ka wo tej to dekho, Arjun par bhi wo bhaari hai. Chakravyuh sa hai ye jag, par Abhimanyu tu banega kyun? Jab Krishna tere saarthi hain, toh parajit tu rahega kyun?
