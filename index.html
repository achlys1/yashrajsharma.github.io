Kalyug Ke Shatranj Mein

Is Kalyug ke shatranj mein, Krishna ki Leela bhari hai, Ram ke niyamon ka shasan, Is abhimaani Kalyug par jaari hai. Apne netra-patal par dekho, Kalyug ka charitra dikh jayega, Krishna ki kriya ka sagar, Saanson mein ghul jayega. Kahne ko to pyade hain hum, Jinda jalti lashein hain hum, Kitna hai abhiman to dekho, Apne andar Ram to dekho. Wazir ki satta jaari hai, Ab ghode ki bhi baari hai, Baano ki shayya par lete, Nirmal kasht ki baari hai. Karna ne dhanush uthaya hai, Ab mahima jag ne jaani hai. Oonth ki chaal hai tedhi magar, Ye haathi bada ahankari hai, In nanhe se pyadon ko dekho, Jo raja par bhi bhari hai. Kalyug ke is shatranj mein, Krishna ki nirmal vaani hai, Karna ka wo tej to dekho, Arjun par bhi wo bhaari hai. Chakravyuh sa hai ye jag, par Abhimanyu tu banega kyun? Jab Krishna tere saarthi hain, toh parajit tu rahega kyun? Shatranj ki bisat par bhale hi mohre kaath ke hon, Par kalyug ke is rann mein, rath tere hausle ka chalega. Raja ko girte dekha hai, pyado ko ladte dekha hai, Maine Kalyug mein insaan ko, aadha jeete-marte dekha hai. Par yaad rahe... Ant mein na maut hogi, na Karna par haar bhari hai, Kalyug ke is shatranj mein, sirf Karm ki baari hai.
